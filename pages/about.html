Who We Are

GoldRate Interior Decor is a retail store offering eclectic as well as custom made premium quality home decor accents & essentials to consumers who want to create a living space that looks and feels amazing.

Our Mission

Transforming creative ideas and descriptions from customers into beautiful and meaningful custom pillow designs which both the recipient and giver appreciate.

Why Us?

By working with customers in the design phase, we give them the satisfaction of having input into their piece of art, without the intense investment of their own time and labour that would be necessary to produce it themselves and still end up feeling like they did it. The result is always personalized,usable,beautiful piece that will be appreciated for generations to come.
